100 % Natural Ingredient

There are just two basic ingredients needed to make real chocolate: cocoa beans and sugar.Cocoa Beans the foundation of all chocolate, cocoa beans come from the Theobroma cacao tree, native to South America.Sugar, sweetens the chocolate and can come from various sources like cane sugar, beet sugar, or even coconut sugar. The type of sugar can affect the taste of the final chocolate product
